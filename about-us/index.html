The Energy to Make an Impact

Rudhra Solar Greentech is a premier solar O&M company founded in 2016. We provide advanced, fully autonomous robotic services — cost-effectively maximising the performance of utility-scale PV sites all over India.

With over 8 years of expertise, our outstanding team of engineers, technicians, and operations professionals bring to the market the most safe and reliable solar maintenance services — designed to meet any challenge, supported by experienced O&M specialists.

We continue to grow as one of India's most trusted solar energy management partners, offering the widest range of robotic cleaning and on-ground manpower services in the market.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted and innovative solar energy management company — driving the clean energy revolution through technology, reliability, and an uncompromising commitment to performance.

We envision a future where every solar asset operates at its maximum potential, every day — enabling our clients and the planet to thrive together sustainably.

Our Mission

To deliver world-class solar O&M services that maximise energy yield and profitability for our clients — through cutting-edge robotics, skilled manpower, and data-driven operations excellence.

  • Deploy advanced autonomous cleaning technology
  • Build India's most skilled solar workforce
  • Reduce water usage through dry robotic cleaning
  • Deliver measurable ROI for every client
